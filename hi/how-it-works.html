ZYE is a comprehensive booking platform that brings multiple services under one app. Whether you need a cab, food delivery, flight booking, medicine, movie tickets, or home services - ZYE provides a seamless booking experience with transparent pricing and quality service partners.

ZYE currently operates in Bengaluru and Chennai, with cab/auto services fully available. We're expanding to Kolkata, Nagpur, and Indore soon, with additional services being rolled out gradually in each city.

Yes, you'll need to create a free account to use ZYE services. This helps us provide a personalized experience, save your preferences, and ensure secure transactions.

Simply open the ZYE app, select the service you need, enter your requirements (location, items, etc.), review the details and price, then confirm your booking. You can track your booking in real-time within the app.

ZYE accepts multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, digital wallets, and cash (for select services). All digital payments are secured with industry-standard encryption.
